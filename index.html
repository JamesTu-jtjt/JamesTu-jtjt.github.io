About Me

I am Eng-Shen (James) Tu, a Software Engineer at Appier and a research intern in the Software Engineering Group at Cornell University, advised by Assistant Professor Saikat Dutta. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Taiwan.

My work centers on AI for Software Engineering, including agentic and retrieval-augmented workflows, evaluation for LLM-based software agents, and automated software testing. I develop systems that improve reliability, software quality, and developer productivity.
